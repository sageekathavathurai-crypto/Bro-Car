Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does RC transfer take?
RC transfer typically takes 5–7 working days. We handle all formalities and keep you updated.
Can I get a car loan through Bro Cars?
Yes! We work with trusted banks and NBFCs. Approval usually takes 24–48 hours with minimal documentation.
Is test drive available for all cars?
Yes, test drives are available for all "Available" cars. Call or WhatsApp us to book a time slot.
Do you buy cars in any condition?
We evaluate all cars regardless of condition. Contact us for a free evaluation.
What documents are needed to buy a car?
You'll need Aadhaar card, PAN card, and address proof. For loans, income proof may also be required.
